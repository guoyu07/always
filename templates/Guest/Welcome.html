The Student Memorial Program remembers and honors Appalachian students who have passed away while enrolled as a student. Each student is memorialized on a plaque in the B.B. Dougherty Administration Building. Appalachian annually invites each of these students' families to campus in the Fall for a special Student Memorial Program. This program honors and remembers each of these Mountaineers by reading their names and words of remembrance from the family.

These students will "Always be a Mountaineer."
